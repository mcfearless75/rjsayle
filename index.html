About Us

Absolute Perfection
in Every Job

R.J. Sayle Plumbing & Heating Ltd specialises in all commercial, light commercial, shopfitting, large-format homes and domestic plumbing & heating applications.

We have vast on-site experience in workmanship, system design and industry knowledge. We work closely with our clients, offering guidance and recommendations to develop the most up-to-date, energy-efficient systems available — ensuring a smooth, first-class customer service is experienced throughout.

Our expertise is unrivalled in boiler installation, heating systems, unvented hot water systems, boosted cold water systems, and hot, cold and above-ground drainage pipework. We offer strict fixed-price contracts for peace of mind and always deliver on time, guaranteed.
